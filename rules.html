You and the computer will both start off with random stats. You have the choice to attack or defend. If you defend and the computer's attack doesn't do damage to you, then you regain some fatigue which is your health. If both you and the computer defend you will both gain some fatigue. One the computer's fatigue is 0 or below, you can use your finishing move. You can also use it when your fatigue is more than double the computer's. However, the same rules apply to the computer. A finishing move, is a one hit KO move. It will 100% land and whoever delivers it will win the game!
